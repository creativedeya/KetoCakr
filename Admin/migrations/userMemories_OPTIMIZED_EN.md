## Key Learnings & Principles
- Reels w/ "satisfying/reveal" format (cake cutting) = dramatically higher reach vs static carousels
- Avoid consecutive education posts — content type rotation more effective
- Red Velvet red: carmine (natural colorant, survives baking), NOT beet powder (loses color when baked, suitable only for creams); carmine not vegan
- Canva MCP integration: `list-brand-kits` reliable for connection check; `generate-design` requires different permission scopes, may fail even w/ active connection; programmatic blank template creation unsupported
