# KetoCakeLab — Voice Profile EN (Compressed)
*Reference for English Instagram captions that sound human, not AI*

---

## Core Tension
Deyana's natural voice: poetic, earthy, personal. Instagram English: punchy, direct, hashtag-heavy. KetoCakeLab voice lives **between** — warmth + authenticity of Bulgarian writing filtered through rhythm of good English copy.

---

## Voice Characteristics
- **Confident, not pushy.** States things. Doesn't sell.
- **Specific, not vague.** Real numbers, textures, moments — not "incredible/amazing/game-changing."
- **Warm, not gushing.** Reader feels seen, not flattered.
- **Curious, not lecturing.** Shares discovery, doesn't deliver verdict.
- **Feminine but not girlish.** Elegant restraint, not exclamation marks.

---

## Caption Formula (Mandatory)

Every Instagram caption follows this:

1. **Opening line** — specific, image-based, or tension-based. Never restates photo. Never starts w/ "I."
2. **Body** — 2–4 sentences. Story or insight. Specific detail.
3. **LAB NOTE** — `LAB NOTE — [fact/tip/hack]`
4. **CTA** — `Comment LAB for free Keto Baking Cheat Sheet.`
5. **Link CTA** — `Full protocol → ketocakelab.com` (pre-launch) / `Full protocol in the app →` (post-launch)
6. **Tagline** — italic Unicode English sentence as tagline (NOT Bulgarian anymore)
7. **Sign-off** — `𝑊𝑒𝑙𝑐𝑜𝑚𝑒 𝑡𝑜 𝐾𝑒𝑡𝑜𝐶𝑎𝑘𝑒 𝐿𝑎𝑏`
8. **Hashtags** — max 3: 1 broad + 1 niche + `#ketocakelab`

---

## Opening Lines

✅ DO:
- Start w/ scene: "3.2g net carbs. One slice. No compromise."
- Start w/ number: "47 components. Infinite combinations."
- Start w/ tension: "The cake that made me stop saying 'I can't have that.'"
- Start w/ unexpected observation

❌ NEVER:
- "I am so excited to share..."
- "Today I want to talk about..."
- "Have you ever wondered..."
- Restate what's in photo

## Hashtag Strategy (Max 3)

Place in caption, not first comment.

`#ketodessert` · `#lowcarbdessert` · `#ketobaking` · `#sugarfreedessert`

**Branded — ALWAYS use:**

## Alt Text (SEO Keywords)

Alt text helps Instagram algorithm + Google (indexes posts since July 2025). Write as natural sentence, not keyword list.

**Formula:** [visual description] + [key ingredient/detail] + [health keyword]

**Core keywords (weave naturally):**
- `keto cake` · `low carb dessert` · `sugar free cake` · `almond flour cake`
- `keto cheesecake` · `no sugar added` · `keto chocolate cake` · `low carb baking`
- `diabetic friendly dessert` · `blood sugar friendly` · `low glycemic dessert`
- `PCOS friendly` · `insulin resistance diet` · `keto dessert components`
- `build your own keto cake` · `modular keto dessert`

**Examples:**
- *"Dark chocolate keto cake w/ almond flour crust, 3g net carbs/slice, sugar free"*
- *"Layered keto cheesecake w/ berry filling, low carb + diabetic friendly dessert"*
- *"Keto dessert components — chocolate base, vanilla cream, raspberry filling, built like puzzle"*

❌ NEVER keyword stuff: "keto cake dessert sugar free low carb chocolate almond"

## Phrases That Belong
- "Built like a puzzle."
- "No compromise."
- "This is what keto actually tastes like."
- "Pick your base. Pick your cream. Make it yours."
- Direct, short declarative sentences
- Sensory details: texture, temperature, color, net carbs

---

## NEVER Use

**Banned keto/food clichés:**
- "Guilt-free" → NEVER
- "Clean eating" → NEVER
- "Wholesome treat" → NEVER
- "Indulge without guilt" → NEVER
- "You deserve this" → NEVER
- "So good you won't believe it's keto" → cliché
- "This one's a keeper!" → NEVER

**Banned AI/influencer language:**
- "Game-changer/game-changing" → name specific change
- "Unlock your potential" → describe specific outcome
- "Trust me on this one" → NEVER
- "Let's dive in" → cut it
- "I'm obsessed" → too influencer
- "Drop a 🍰 if you want recipe!" → NEVER
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Emoji per line

**Banned structures:**
- Opening by restating topic
- Ending w/ motivational quote
- Three-point sermon structure
- "Both sides" hedging — have opinion
- Summary ending ("In conclusion...")

---

## Emoji Policy
- Max 2–3 emoji per caption
- Only when they add meaning, not decoration
- Preferred: 🌐 (always in sign-off), occasional 🍫 🍓 🧁 when directly relevant
- Never: emoji per line, emoji as bullet points

## English Tagline (Replaces Bulgarian Sentence)

Every caption includes English italic sentence as tagline before sign-off. This is non-negotiable — signature of authenticity, moment real person behind brand appears.

- Should carry emotional weight, not just translate what already said
- Can be poetic, personal, or simply warm
- Appears before sign-off
- Example: *Made with intention and very dark chocolate.*

**IMPORTANT:** Bulgarian sentence REMOVED from caption formula. Replaced w/ English italic tagline to avoid confusion w/ Russian language.

---

## 7-Point Check Before Publishing

1. Could anyone have written this? If yes — add specific detail from KetoCakeLab world
2. Any banned phrase? Search + replace
3. Opens by restating topic? Rewrite opening
4. Ends w/ summary or inspirational quote? Cut last line
5. Every claim specific? No vague superlatives
6. English italic tagline before sign-off? Add it
7. Read out loud. Sounds like person or brand?
